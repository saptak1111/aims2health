Our Story (in brief)

In July 2018, five of us (of the six current Directors of AIMS2Health Pvt Ltd who trained at AIIMS, New Delhi in the 1970s) met to discuss how to bring under ‘one virtual roof’ all doctors trained at AIIMS, New Delhi, who are currently in the private sector.

One AIIMS, New Delhi cannot meet all the complex healthcare needs even of all Indians residing within a 25 km radius of Delhi. The opening of a few more AIIMS at a few locations around the country is also insufficient to serve the needs of all Indians. Our initial discussion set in motion a process that led to a telemedicine start-up, which we registered in December 2018, as a private limited company with headquarters in Delhi. The main focus of the services of AIMS2Health is to provide second opinion services.AIMS2Health Private Limited incorporated with MCA on 19 December 2018.
